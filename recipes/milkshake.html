Quick Banana Milkshake

Home

A delicious banana shake to try if you don't have a lot of time or ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 3 ice cubes
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 banana
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine ice, milk, banana, sugar and cinnamon in a blender. Blend until smooth
